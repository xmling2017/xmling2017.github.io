信号语言

  1. 代词法 不推荐使用it，原因是，很容易指代不明。一般是these/this加上前一句的内容
  2. 冒号法/从句法

    • 表示原因

      The experiment was unsuccessful __ the measuring……

      due to(thefact that)/ on account of(thefact that)/in view of (thefact that)/as /because^[because 不能用于句首] /since

    • 表示结果

      The measuring instruments were calibrated accurately, __ the experiment was successful.

      therefore/ as a result (of which)/ consequently which is why/ hence/ so^[不要以so开头，不正式；hence, therefore是副词，不能用于连接两个句子，一般用法, and therefore或: therefore]

    • 表示对比/差异

      British students are all vegetarians, __ Norwegian students eat meat every day.

      however/ on the other hand/ whereas/ thile/ but/ by contrast^[on the contrary和conversely表示反方面是正确的，使用前需考虑语境]

    • 表示出乎意料

      1. __ it was difficult, a solution was eventually found. (Although /Even though /Though)
      2. __ the difficulty, a solution was eventually found. (Despite /In spite of /Regardless of /Notwithstanding)
      3. It was difficult; __ a solution was eventually found. (nevertheless /however /yet /nonetheless /even so)

    • 表示“增补”或“递进”

      We used a batch processing system because it was more effective; __ it was faster.

      增补 递进
      in addition also
      moreover secondly(etc.)
      furthermore in the second place(etc.)
      apart from that/ which what is more

      besides 语义比较强，一般用于更有说服力的句子中
